## How It Works

1. **Enter Your Pixel IDs** – Copy and paste from your ad platforms into your dashboard
2. **We Track Everything** – Customer views, cart additions, and purchases are tracked automatically
3. **See Results in Your Ad Manager** – Conversion data appears in Facebook, Google, TikTok, and Snapchat

No technical knowledge required. Takes 2 minutes to set up.
